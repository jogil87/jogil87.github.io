My Ingredients for Success

Technical

I am not a developer but I speak the language and can confidently research different technologies and web-services and recognize their potential utility in a product. I have hands on experience using git, sql, mongo, html/css, CURL, linux CLI, and a variety of other tech including UI/UX tools, DNS, CMS admin tools, SEO, PM tools, and more.

CREATOR/INNOVATOR

I am an innovator focused on crafting the simplest available solution to solve business and customer challenges. My experience has taught me that if it is not stable, and you can't maintain it, it is worthless. More than ever, the web is about great data and great user experiences, I pride myself on understanding how to bring both to create great products.

Strategic Leader

I have experience getting buy-in for new products and can engage and motivate stakeholders, both internal (executives, developers, sales), and external (integrators, partners, clients, end-users)
